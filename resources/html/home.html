Welcome to eFinanceTracker!

What is eFinanceTracker?

eFinanceTracker was designed to help people manage their personal finances, without spending a ton of money to do so. Once you get setup, you will be able to track your income and expenses, savings, investments, spending habits, and so much more!

Unlike other financial management solutions, eFinanceTracker is being designed to be affordable to everybody. It does not make sense to us why people should have to pay a small fortune every month in order to manage their own money. Not everybody can afford that. Our goal is to make eFinanceTracker affordable, regardless of your income. Though we haven't yet set a final price, we have decided that the subscription price will be less than $1.00 per day, and we are figuring out ways to make that number drop as well. Once a final price is agreed upon, we will announce it here.

This web site will not communicate with your other financial websites, such as your banks or your employers. You must add your information manually. You decide how much detail that you want to add, but the more detail you give, the better you will be able to understand your financial situation.
